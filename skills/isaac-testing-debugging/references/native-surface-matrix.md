## Hard Rules

- One working surface never proves a second native surface shares its asset or
  registration route.
- Record the exact requested surface before searching resources.
- For collectibles, distinguish colored `gfxroot + gfx` art from `deathanm2`
  death portraits.
- For pockets, distinguish pickup art, pocket HUD key, cardfront, and optional
  EID icon.
- For characters, distinguish select, co-op, in-run HUD, boss, death, costume,
  and completion surfaces.
- For progression, distinguish persistence, availability, reward, popup, and
  completion-mark surfaces.
- A static path check cannot prove a native engine surface rendered; list a
  separate in-game check for every requested surface.
