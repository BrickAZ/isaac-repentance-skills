# Pool Placement And Availability

Choose a pool from the item's acquisition story, not a generic power label.

Ask:

- What player decision, room type, or risk should lead to finding this item?
- Does the item reinforce the identity of that pool?
- Is it broadly useful, situational, run-defining, or an intentional gamble?
- Would adding it to this pool make other existing items less visible without a good reason?

## Review Rule

For every included pool, write one sentence explaining why the item belongs there. For every tempting excluded pool, record why it stays out. This makes "put it everywhere" visible as a design decision rather than an accident.

## Unlocks

Pool placement is default availability. Unlocks, challenge rewards, scripted grants, and transformations are separate routes and must not be silently substituted for a pool entry.
